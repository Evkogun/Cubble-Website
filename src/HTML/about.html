About Cubble

Connecting people, not just profiles.

Cubble is reimagining what it means to be social online. We believe true connection isn’t built on endless scrolling or hollow likes, it’s built through real conversations, honest expression and the freedom to be yourself.

Our Story

Cubble was born from something deeply personal: loneliness.

Jessica Miller, our founder, saw how social media and dating apps promised connection but often left people feeling more isolated than ever. Scrolling through curated likes or swiping through endless profiles, she noticed something was missing: real, meaningful human connection.

Rather than accept that this was “just the way things are,” she imagined something better - a space designed to help people truly see and be seen. A space where conversations matter more than clicks, and where people connect not for status, but for substance.

That vision became Cubble. Today, it’s a growing global community of people who are choosing depth over dopamine and connection over comparison.

Our Mission

At Cubble, we’re on a mission to change the way people connect online.

We are committed to:

  • Putting you in control: Your data belongs to you. Privacy isn’t an afterthought; it’s a foundation.
  • Encouraging real conversations: We prioritize meaningful interactions over mindless scrolling and empty metrics.
  • Creating safe, welcoming spaces: Everyone deserves a supportive environment where they can be their authentic self without fear or judgment.
  • Fighting loneliness: We believe technology should bring people together - not push them further apart.

Our mission isn’t just about building a social platform - it’s about helping to rebuild genuine community in a digital age.

Because connection isn’t a luxury. It’s a necessity.
