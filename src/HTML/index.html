How Cubble Works

Meet in Groups, Not Just Matches

Cubble places you in a private "bubble" with 10 like-minded people for 48 hours. You chat, connect, and see what clicks, no swiping, no endless scrolling.

AI That Looks Out for You

Our on-device AI suggests icebreakers, nudges better conversations, and flags harmful messages before they're ever sent, all without invading your privacy.

From Chat to Real-World Connections

Whether you're looking for new friends or a date, Cubble helps build trust first. Verified video intros and community moderation ensure everyone feels safe showing up.

Built for Students (and Beyond)

Our campus edition connects students using .edu emails in localised bubbles, making it easy to find people in your area. Broader community rollouts coming soon.
